Privacy Policy

Effective: April 20, 2026

are we™ ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What we collect

  • Phone number — used to create your account via one-time SMS verification and to match you to incoming questions.
  • First name — provided by you during sign-up; shown to people you send questions to.
  • Recipient info — when you send a question, we store the selected contact's name and phone number alongside the question, so the answer screen and receipt can identify them. Your contact list as a whole is never uploaded — only the one contact you pick. When you tap "Ask," your phone's native Messages app opens with a pre-filled text; the SMS itself is sent by you through Messages, not by our servers.
  • Answers — the answer the recipient selects is stored so it can be shown to the person who asked.
  • Push notification token — if you grant notification permission, an anonymous device token is stored so we can notify you when someone answers.

How we use it

Your data is used solely to operate the app: matching senders to recipients, delivering questions, and displaying answers. That's it.

Who we share it with

We do not sell your data. We rely on the following service providers to operate the app:

  • Supabase — authentication and database (Postgres).
  • Twilio — sends the one-time SMS code during sign-in.
  • Expo — delivers push notifications to your device.

Each provider processes the minimum data required for its function and is governed by its own privacy practices.

Data retention

Your account and associated data are retained as long as you use the app. You can request deletion at any time by emailing hello@arewe.app.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by emailing us.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at hello@arewe.app.
